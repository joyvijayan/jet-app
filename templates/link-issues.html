8. How to Fix Instagram Link Loading Issues

Sometimes you might face issues while trying to fetch a video. Here are the most common reasons and how to fix them:

  • Private Accounts: InstaJet cannot access videos from private accounts for security and privacy reasons. Make sure the content is public.
  • Invalid URL: Ensure you have copied the full link from the "Copy Link" option in the Instagram app.
  • Server Busy: If many people are downloading at once, try refreshing the page and pasting the link again after a few seconds.
  • Internet Connection: A slow or unstable connection can sometimes interrupt the fetching process.
