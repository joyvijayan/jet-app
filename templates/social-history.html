14. Managing Your Social Media History

Your digital memories are valuable. Managing your social media history effectively helps you keep track of your growth as a creator.

Why Archive Offline? Content on social media platforms can sometimes be removed or lost due to copyright issues or account changes. Archiving your best performing reels locally is a smart move.

Tips for Digital Management:

  • Download your top-performing videos using InstaJet for safe keeping.
  • Organize your downloads into folders based on categories or dates.
  • Regularly review your past content to understand what worked best for your audience.

By keeping a local backup of your favorite moments, you ensure that your digital footprint remains under your control.
