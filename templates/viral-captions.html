5 Secret Tips to Boost Your Views

  1. The 3-Second Hook: Ensure your first 3 seconds are engaging enough to stop the scroll. If viewers scroll past immediately, the algorithm stops pushing your video.
  2. High-Quality Lighting: Instagram favors high-resolution, well-lit videos. Avoid blurry or dark footage.
  3. Consistent Posting: Aim to post at least 3-5 times a week. Consistency builds authority in your niche.
  4. Engage with Comments: Reply to comments in the first hour of posting to boost the engagement rate.
  5. Use Keywords: Write descriptive captions with relevant keywords so your Reel appears in search results.

Understanding the 2026 Algorithm

The current algorithm prioritizes Watch Time and Shares. If people watch your video until the end or send it to their friends, Instagram will show it to thousands of new people.
