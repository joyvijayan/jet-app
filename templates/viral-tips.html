Why Your Instagram Reels Aren't Getting Views?

To go viral in 2026, you need to stay ahead of the algorithm. One of the most important factors is using trending audio within the first 24 hours of its release. This signals to Instagram that your content is fresh and relevant.

Ensure your lighting is good and the first 3 seconds are engaging enough to stop the scroll. The hook is everything! If viewers scroll past immediately, the algorithm will stop pushing your video to others. Consistent posting also plays a huge role in visibility.
