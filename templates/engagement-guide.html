11. Understanding Social Media Engagement

Engagement is the heartbeat of social media. It measures how people interact with your content through likes, comments, and shares.

Why Engagement Matters: High engagement tells the algorithm that your content is valuable, which helps in reaching a wider audience.

How to Boost Interaction:

  • Post consistently at the right times.
  • Ask questions in your captions.
  • Reply to comments to build a community.

Using InstaJet to analyze viral videos can give you great ideas on what kind of content gets the most engagement in your niche!
